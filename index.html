About Amalia

Amalia Hay is currently an office assistant at BioTox Laboratories, proudly providing forensic toxicology services to the counties of Riverside, San Bernardino and San Diego. While she enjoys the fast pace and diversity this career provides, she is looking forward to her completion of the UCR coding bootcamp program and applying her skills as a full stack developer.

Prior to working at BioTox, Amalia spent over 6 years as a certified nurse assistant. Like all hardworking caregivers, she learned to multitask like an octopus knitting a cozy sweater, while cooking a 5 course meal, and compulsively dancing the samba. She also learned that a strong team and positive attitude are key to overcoming any challenge.

When she isn't scouring the wealth of information available online to developers of the 21st century, Amalia enjoys baking tasty treats, camping and swimming (preferably in the ocean).
